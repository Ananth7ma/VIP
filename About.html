Story

VIP Bio was established in the year 1999 by Subramanian Palaniappan( Employee turned entrepreneur) and Dr. Vasanthi Palaniappan (Scientist in Herbal Biotechnology).She holds master degree in Bio Chemistry with Ph.D in Animal Science from Hyderabad Central University(HCU).

  • The journey of VIP Bio began with founder who has experience in domain of Poultry since 1988 and Aqua since 2003.
  • Within couple of years starting the entrepreneurial journey in manufacturing, realization happened to build Organization on the bed of INNOVATIONS.
  • VIP Bio focuses on manufacturing and distribution of a wide range of natural, chemical-free phytogenic feed supplements that deliver holistic health for the livestock industry.

Our Vision

  • Partner in Organic Food Chain production for a Healthier Nation.

Our Mission

  • Maintaining our dedication to the sustainable development of our industry by Functional Innovations backed by Phytogenics.
  • Commitment to integrity in serving our clients and strive to offer the most value based and comprehensive care available.

Product development

  • Maintaining our dedication to the sustainable We strongly believe that product ideas should germinate from field through frequent interactions with stakeholders more importantly Farmers with a special understanding of their problems.
  • We work around knowledge of the solutions using traditional knowledge coupled with scientific approach.
  • We rely on indigenous raw materials, keep the process less complicated, product outcome performance easily measurable and finally align with eco system in all aspects.
  • We never burden the end user with abnormal cost in the name of solution since our core concept is value for money.
  • We give priority for scientific validation of products and keep documenting the feed back of end users year after year.
  • We support actively ,Private label service(B2B) to companies looking to outsource manufacturing and there by developing their own brand.
